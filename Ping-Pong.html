Rules:

Ping-pong will count upto your number from 1 with the following exception:

  1. Number divisible by 3 are replaced with 'Ping'
  2. Number divisible by 5 are replaced with 'Pong'
  3. Number divisible by 15 are replaced with'pingpong'
